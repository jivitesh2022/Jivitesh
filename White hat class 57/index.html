Hi!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invnted the internet (or the World Wide Web) in 1989 while at CERN, the European Particle Physics Laboratory. He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a professor in Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford. In 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal society and in 2004 he was Knighted.

In 1994 Berners-Lee founded the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) at the laboratory of Computer Science at the Massachutts Institute of Technology in Boscon. It was made up of companies wanted to work together to create web standards and also improve the web. In 1995 Microsoft produced the first version of Internet Explorer and this Commercial brower created enormous growth in websites and web users. The web was first use for e-commerce in 1997.
